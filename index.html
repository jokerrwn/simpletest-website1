Collaboration

THE CONCEPT OF COLLABORATION

The concept of collaboration is to allow computer scientists to bring together skills and ideas into one big program.

ㅤ

WHY YOU SHOULD COLLABORATE

Collaboration is important in CS to avoid personal bias while making specific programs alone.

ㅤ

Without further ado,

5 BENEFICIAL FACTORS OF COLLABORATION
Reason Facts
Speed and Efficiency While collaborating, your team will work quickly and take the parts that they are strong at, which means they’ll be able to create a great part of the program because they are good at it
Shared Ideas While collaborating, you and your team will know what you guys want, and know what you guys don’t want.
New, Creative Ideas While collaborating, everyone’s unique experience will cause them to think differently from one another, so many creative ideas can be formed in groups
Quick debugging While collaborating, your team will all be working at once, which can lead to finding bugs faster than you could ever do by yourself.
Reduced stress While collaborating, stress can be reduced because members of your team can choose parts of code that suit their strengths, skills, and talents.

ㅤ

5 ESSENTIAL SKILLS LEANED FROM COLLABORATION
Reason Facts
Communication Allows you to talk to your peers, gradually, you’ll be able to communicate better
Teamwork Being able to work effectively with others,
Leadership Taking the initiative and speaking loudly about something you believe would be a good idea
Adaptibility Adjusting to work styles and unexpected challenges
Conflict Resolution Respectfully disagreeing with others, reaching compromises

ONLINE TOOLS USED FOR COLLABORATION

Discussion

Zoom, Messages, Email

Online tools that can allow you to communicate with your team and talk any time, anywhere.



Documentation

Docs, Microsoft Paint

Online tools that allow you to document progress and plans for the program with your team.

STATE WHICH BENEFIT AND COLLABORATION SKILL ARE MOST SIGNIFICANT AND WHY

ㅤ


BENEFIT
As a benefit, creative ideas are the most significant because implementing creative ideas can make your program unique from others, show off the skills that came from your team, and bring in a diverse group of people who are interested in your program.

ㅤ


SKILL
A skill that is most important would be communication. Clear communication can help you avoid losing ideas, and various misunderstandings. Good communication allows everyone to understand the goal of the project, share progress and coordinate with each other.
